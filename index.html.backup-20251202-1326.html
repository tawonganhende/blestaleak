Company Overview

Blestar Leak Private Limited is a rapidly growing construction company established in November 2019. We provide comprehensive construction, structural engineering and roof waterproofing services for residential, commercial and industrial projects. Our approach combines industry best practices with high-quality materials to deliver durable, compliant and cost-effective solutions.

Our team conducts thorough site assessments and implements tailored solutions that meet the unique requirements of each project while adhering to local building codes and safety standards.

Mission: Deliver top-quality construction, engineering and waterproofing solutions that exceed client expectations through innovation, skilled workmanship and clear communication.
